Line-for-line torch translation of the Newton-PCG solver. Reproduces NumPy bit-for-bit and passes every cal_18 gate in float64. On a single high-res solve the GPU is not faster (one-time overhead dominates), but the steady per-step cost is 4.7× lower — training is the favorable regime.
